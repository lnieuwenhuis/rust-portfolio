About

Less spectacle, more useful surface area.

Lars likes taking a workflow apart until the state, permissions, and next action are visible. The best projects are the ones where a user stops thinking about the interface because the path through it makes sense.

Most of the work so far sits around React, TypeScript, Laravel, Vue, and GraphQL. This portfolio is the Rust experiment in public: Axum for routing, Askama for templates, SQLx for the database, and PostgreSQL for the project archive.
